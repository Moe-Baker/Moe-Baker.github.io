Zombie Party

A multiplayer game created for Goethe Institute's IMAGINE festival in Sudan.
The game utilizes the player's own phone to control characters on screen, players will scan a QR code that will open up a web-application that will allow them to join the game.
Made with Unity & Cocos Creator.
